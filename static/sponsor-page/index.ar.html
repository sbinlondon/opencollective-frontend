Legally support communities that don't have a legal entity.

International or local communities often don't have a legal entity. Open Collective enables you to financially support those communities by working with fiscal sponsors who can provide your company with an invoice.

Transparency

You’ll always know what your contributions are being used for, in real time.

Get an invoice for your donations.

Supporting communities can be an accounting nightmare. Most of them don’t have a legal entity and therefore they cannot give you an invoice for your donation. Open Collective solves that by hosting collectives that fall in the same category on one single legal entity.

Consolidated invoices.

Get once a month a consolidated invoice for all your donations to all collectives that are hosted on the same legal entity. Your accounting department will thank you for that!

Purchasing orders

Your company has a special process for managing vendors? No problem. We have you covered. We do it at a host (fiscal sponsor) level, because it doesn’t make sense to go through such a heavy process for every single community (collective).

Reporting

Keeping track of your donations to dozens if not hundreds of collectives is hard. With Open Collective you receive a monthly report that aggregates all your participations in one place.
